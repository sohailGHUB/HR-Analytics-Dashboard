# HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## Overview

This project is an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard developed using Microsoft Power BI to analyze 
employee attrition and workforce trends. The dashboard provides key insights into employee demographics, 
salary distribution, job roles, overtime, education, and years at the company to help HR teams make informed decisions.

## Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition across different dimensions.
- Identify departments and job roles with high attrition.
- Understand the relationship between salary and employee turnover.
- Examine the impact of overtime on attrition.
- Monitor key HR metrics through an interactive dashboard.

## Key Insights

- Total Employees: **1,480**
- Employees Left: **238**
- Attrition Rate: **16.1%**
- Average Employee Age: **37 Years**
- Average Monthly Salary: **6.5K**
- Average Years at Company: **7.01 Years**

### Business Insights

- Laboratory Technicians experienced the highest employee attrition.
- Employees earning below **5K** had the highest attrition.
- Employees working overtime were more likely to leave the organization.
- Employees aged **26–35 years** contributed the highest number of attrition cases.
- Most employees left within the first few years of joining the company.
